### 🚀 Featured Projects

These are some projects, mainly iOS apps, in which I've worked, contributing to many stages of the product development cycle.

*   **WavePong**:
    The greatest classic of all time, now in its most accessible and challenging version. Developed at Apple Developer Academy | UFPE. WavePong is an immersive, retro-wave sensory game where players must rely on their senses and agility.
    To hit the ball, players are guided by sound waves passing between headphones and haptic vibrations emitted upon impact, making it highly inclusive for people with visual or hearing impairments. As the difficulty increases, a large wave obscures the ball, requiring players to rely entirely on their hearing and touch.
    *   **Built with:** 100% Swift, SwiftUI, and SpriteKit.
    *   **My Contribution:** Co-developer. I collaborated within a five-person development team.
